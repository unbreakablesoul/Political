Political Updates

Stay informed with the latest government announcements and political news.

Polavaram Dam Project

The Water Resources Department has begun removing the temporary guide wall at the Polavaram project site. This will expose the main diaphragm wall and allow for the construction of the main dam to proceed.

🗓️ Oct 14, 2025

Deepam-2 Scheme Implementation

The Civil Supplies Department is continuing the implementation of the Deepam-2 scheme, which provides three free LPG refills to eligible rice cardholders. As of early October, 4,52,394 beneficiaries in the district had been identified.

🗓️ Oct 8, 2025

District Review Meeting

District Collector Chadalavada Nagarani presided over a review meeting for the District Rural Development Agency (DRDA) and the Animal Husbandry Department. The Collector announced that 1,419 units were sanctioned under the annual credit plan for the district and encouraged women from self-help groups (SHGs) to use these loans for economic development.

🗓️ oct 7, 2025

Weather and flooding in Eluru

Heavy rainfall caused extensive waterlogging in low-lying areas of Eluru and West Godavari. In Eluru, municipal officials were instructed to clear drains and level roads to prevent further inconvenience to residents.

🗓️ Oct 14, 2025

Doctors' protests

Healthcare staff across the district wore black badges to work to express solidarity with doctors striking for the resolution of various issues. Protestors gathered during their lunch breaks.

🗓️ Oct 14, 2025

Cybercrime awareness

Officials warned residents in towns like Bhimavaram, Tanuku, and Akividu about the rise of sophisticated cybercrimes, including deepfake and voice-cloning scams.

🗓️ oct 14, 2025

Rainfall warning

The Andhra Pradesh State Disaster Management Authority (APSDMA) issued an alert for moderate to heavy rainfall with thunderstorms in West Godavari and other districts until October 18.

🗓️ oct 14, 2025

Sanitation drive

A "Swarnandhra-Swachhandhra" sanitation program was held at the Gubbalamangamma Temple in Kamavaram village.

🗓️ Oct 14, 2025

Eluru provisional merit list

The GMC, Eluru, published provisional merit lists for 19 cadres and announced that grievances would be accepted until this date.
